I'm Alessio, a self-taught developer from Italy who got hooked on programming at 16 after trying — and failing — to mod a video game. That obsession turned into a career. I went from writing spaghetti Java to building full-stack web apps, mobile applications, and AI-powered products used by real people.

Over the years I've worked across the whole stack: Django APIs, React frontends, React Native apps, browser extensions, and LLM integrations. My sweet spot is the intersection of AI and practical software — tools that actually save people time, not just impress in a demo. I'm driven by clean architecture, fast iteration, and shipping things that work.
